I dont't really lie

Published 2 days ago5 min read

I don't really lie,
but I lied that very day,
guess I can't accept cos am too shy.
That evening I was with her,
I was happy & full of gay,
I don't really lie,
but she looked gorgeous with that pink fur,
that I can't take my eyes away,
guess I can't accept cos am too shy.
Praising her beauty I felt, am connoisseur,
to appreciate her beauty I gave her a bouquet,
I don't really lie,
I said am not a beauty admirer,
but on your beauty I can write an essay,
guess I can't accept cos am too shy.
Being with her I was full of ardor,
I was mesmerized by her eyes & her sway,
I don't really lie,
guess I can't accept cos am too shy.
