We are together you & me

Published 2 days ago5 min read

We are together
you & me
I am like a flower
and you are a bee.
Take all my nectar away
and make some honey
just like you take away
all my problematic factor
and make it sweet in a way, like honey.
You are high as sky
I am earthly deep
just to meet each other
see how hard we steep.
Maybe it's a delusion
or horizon they say
destined to meet each other
just to push further away.
A never ending tale
our love won't ever fade
you be my jouful melody
I'll be your twilight serenade.
We are together
you & me
being in love forever
while waiting to be free
