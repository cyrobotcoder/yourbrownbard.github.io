A boy is brought to the life.

Published 2 days ago5 min read

A boy is brought to the life,
let's see how hard it is for him to survive.
To thrive, how hard he needs to strive for his life.
He shall learn the ethics of living,
he shall learn the art of giving.
He shall grow kind & wise,
his heart should be clear as wells of paradise.
He must learn & he must burn,
for his living he shall earn.
He lives on & the world grows old,
till his experience has grown,
and his heart turned cold.
He shall collect,
what is costly good, not bought or sold,
the experience, for his life to mould.
He just smiles to the world for show,
but the smile is buried under his woes,
and the eternal grieve he knows.
He grows formal with men & with women polite,
and distrustful of both, when they are out of sight.
H
e lives life, fearing the death he thrives,
but he shall walk on the sharp edged knife,
till, in the death lake he dives.
